- 2024-06-27: [PostgreSQL and Pgvector: Now Faster Than Pinecone, 75% Cheaper, and 100% Open Source](https://www.timescale.com/blog/pgvector-is-now-as-fast-as-pinecone-at-75-less-cost/)
