> 有錄音檔，怎麼轉成 Word Transcript？

可以的，而且方法不少，取決於你**用什麼工具、錄音語言、準確度需求**。我用最實用的方式幫你整理👇

✅ 最簡單的方法（推薦）
------------

### 方法一：**直接用 Word 轉錄（Microsoft 365）**

如果你有 **Microsoft 365（Word 線上版）**：

**步驟**

1.  打開 **Word（網頁版）**
2.  新文件 →「**聽寫**」→「**轉錄**」
3.  上傳錄音檔（mp3 / wav / m4a）
4.  等待轉錄完成
5.  一鍵插入到 Word，還可手動編輯

**優點**

-   直接變成 Word 文件
-   支援 **中文（繁體）**
-   有時間戳、分段清楚

**限制**

-   需要 Microsoft 365
-   單檔大小與長度有限制

> could you guide me the step-by-step instruction using Microsoft 365 Word Web to upload a MP3 and genereate the transcript?

Absolutely 🙂 Here’s a **clear, step‑by‑step guide** to generate a transcript from an **MP3 file using Microsoft 365 Word (Web version)**.

***

## ✅ What you need before starting

*   An active **Microsoft 365** subscription
*   A supported browser (Edge or Chrome works best)
*   An audio file (**.mp3**, .wav, .m4a are supported)
*   Internet connection (transcription runs in the cloud)

***

## 🧭 Step‑by‑Step: Transcribe MP3 in Word Web

### **Step 1: Open Word on the web**

1.  Go to **<https://www.office.com>**
2.  Sign in with your Microsoft 365 account
3.  Click **Word**
4.  Select **Blank document**

***

### **Step 2: Open the Transcribe panel**

1.  In the top ribbon, click **Home**
2.  Look for the **Dictate (🎤)** button
3.  Click the **small arrow ▼ next to Dictate**
4.  Select **Transcribe**

👉 A **Transcribe panel** will open on the right side

***

### **Step 3: Upload your MP3 file**

1.  In the Transcribe panel, click **Upload audio**
2.  Choose your **MP3 file** from your computer
3.  Wait for the upload to complete

⏳ Transcription time depends on audio length (e.g., a 1‑hour file may take several minutes)

***

### **Step 4: Review the transcript**

Once processing finishes, you’ll see:

*   Text broken into segments
*   **Timestamps**
*   Sometimes **speaker labels** (Speaker 1, Speaker 2)

You can:

*   Click any text to **jump to that point in the audio**
*   Edit text directly in the panel if needed

***

### **Step 5: Insert transcript into the Word document**

At the bottom of the Transcribe panel, click **Add to document**

You’ll be asked how to insert it:

*   **Text only**
*   **Text with timestamps**
*   **Text with speakers and timestamps** ✅ (recommended)

Choose your preferred option → transcript is inserted into the Word document.

***

### **Step 6: Edit and save**

*   Format like any Word document (headings, highlights, comments)
*   Click **File → Save As** or it will auto‑save to OneDrive
*   You can download as **.docx** or **.pdf**

***

## ⚠️ Common limitations to be aware of

*   Audio length and monthly transcription minutes are **limited**
*   Works best with **clear audio**
*   Best accuracy for **English and Mandarin Chinese**
*   Requires **Word Web** (not available in Word desktop)

***

## ✅ Tips for better accuracy

*   Use recordings with minimal background noise
*   One speaker at a time improves results
*   If language is mixed (e.g., English + Chinese), accuracy may vary
