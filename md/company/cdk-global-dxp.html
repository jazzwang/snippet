Powering Every Dealership Role

CDK's product suites are designed to support virtually every role within a dealership. The Dealer Management System (DMS) serves as the core, with specialized solutions branching out to cover all aspects of the business.

Connecting the Vehicle Owner Journey

From initial research to long-term ownership, CDK products facilitate a seamless experience for the vehicle buyer by empowering the dealership at every key touchpoint.

1. Shopping & Research

The journey begins online. A potential buyer researches vehicles, compares options, and looks for local inventory.

CDK Connection: Digital Retailing

2. Lead & Engagement

The buyer submits a lead or inquiry. The dealership's sales team receives the information and begins communication.

CDK Connection: CRM

3. The Deal

At the dealership, the sales and F&I teams structure the purchase, including financing, trade-ins, and paperwork.

CDK Connection: Dealer Management System

4. Ownership & Service

The customer owns the vehicle and needs routine maintenance or repairs, scheduling service with the dealership.

CDK Connection: Fixed Operations
