2. Certification Differences

You asked: "Is there a difference between Professional and Foundation?"

Yes, the weighting is significant.

A Professional certification typically holds 3x to 5x the point value of an Associate or Accreditation. The program prioritizes deep technical expertise.

Professional (Data Engineer, ML) High Impact
Associate (Data Analyst, Spark) Moderate Impact
Accreditations (Fundamentals) Low Impact
