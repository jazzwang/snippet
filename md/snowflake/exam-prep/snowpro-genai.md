# SnowPro® Specialty: Gen AI

- The SnowPro® Specialty: Gen AI Certification Exam will validate specialized knowledge, skills, and best practices used to leverage Gen AI methodologies in Snowflake including key concepts, features, and programming constructs.
- https://learn.snowflake.com/en/certifications/snowpro-GenAI/

**SNOWPRO SPECIALTY: GEN AI CERTIFICATION OVERVIEW**

This certification will test the ability to:

- Define and implement Snowflake Gen AI principles, capabilities, and best practices
- Leverage Snowflake Cortex AI features, LLMs, and offerings to meet customer use cases
- Build open-source models with Snowpark Container Services and Snowflake Model Registry

**SNOWPRO SPECIALTY: GEN AI CANDIDATE**

1 or more years of Gen AI experience with Snowflake, in an enterprise environment.

In addition, successful candidates may have experience with writing code in Python. Previous data engineering and SQL knowledge is assumed.
