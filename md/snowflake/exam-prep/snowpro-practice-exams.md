## Practice Exam Policies

Once purchased, candidates will have 24 hours to access and complete their practice exams. The practice exam can only be taken once and cannot be retaken once submitted for scoring. If a candidate does not access the practice exam within the 24-hour window, they will forfeit their registration fee and cannot re-register until 48 hours after the original purchase.
